
# Boycott Game

![Boycott Game picture](bycotte%20game%20img.png)

 **A National, Religious, and Ethical Duty: An educational quiz game to identify products supporting the occupation.** 

 ##  Overview
 This application is a quick, interactive quiz designed to educate users and raise awareness about products and companies linked to or targeted by boycott campaigns. The primary goal is to empower individuals to make responsible and informed purchasing decisions.

## Game Rules 
Upon starting the game for the first time, an initial popup window appears (often with an audio explanation) detailing the rules:
* Speed is Key: You must make a decision before the time bar under the logo runs out.
* The Objective: Answer (❌) if the product is on the boycott list, and (✔️) if it is not. 

 ## How to Play 
 * The game displays a logo and the name of brands. The player must make a fast decision:
* Green Check : For products that are NOT part of the boycott (national products or safe alternatives).
* Red Cross : For products that ARE targeted by the boycott campaigns.
* Scoring: The player's score is calculated based on the speed and accuracy of their answers.
 
 ## Key Features
 **1. Quick and Interactive Quiz :**
 An engaging game format that requires quick wit.

**2. Voiceover for Rules :**
 An initial popup with an audio explanation detailing the game rules.

**2. Score System:**
 To encourage competition and improvement.
